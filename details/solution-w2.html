Widespread Rooftop Solar Water Heating

Promote the installation of solar water heaters on rooftops worldwide to reduce reliance on fossil fuels.

Promote the installation of solar water heaters on rooftops worldwide to reduce reliance on fossil fuels. These systems use sunlight to heat water, cutting energy consumption and emissions. Governments can incentivize adoption through subsidies, tax breaks, and public awareness campaigns. Scalable and cost-effective, this solution leverages existing infrastructure (rooftops) to decarbonize residential and commercial water heating—a major energy demand.
