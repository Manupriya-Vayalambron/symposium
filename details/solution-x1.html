Solution X1: "Xeriscaping for Climate Resilience"

Promote xeriscaping

Promote xeriscaping—water-efficient landscaping using drought-resistant native plants—to reduce urban water use, curb irrigation emissions, and combat heat islands. This low-maintenance approach cuts CO₂ from lawn equipment and conserves freshwater, making cities more climate-adaptive.
